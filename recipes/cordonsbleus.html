Cordons bleus

Cordons bleus are a delicious French dish, made with chicken or turkey, usually chicken. For this recipe we'll use chicken, to make ourselves some delicious homemade cordons bleus!

Ingredients

    Breadcrumbs

  • Panko breadcrumbs
  • Oil spray

    • Meat and flavor

  • 2 small chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper
  • 4 slices swiss cheese
  • 4 to 6 slices of ham
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 2tbsp Dijon mustard

Steps

  1. Reheat oven to 200C/390F (standard) or 180C/350F (fan).
  2. Spread panko breadcrumbs on a baking tray and spray with oil. Bake for 3 minutes or until light golden. Remove and scrape into bowl straight away.
  3. Cut a pocket into each chicken breast, as per the photo below.
  4. Fold the cheese in half and place 2 pieces inside each pocket. Do the same with the ham. Close the pocket, seal with 2 toothpicks. Sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  5. Mix the mayonnaise, mustard, salt and pepper in a bowl. Spread onto the top and sides of the chicken (not underside).
  6. Sprinkle panko over the chicken, using your other hand to press the breadcrumbs onto the side of the chicken. Spray with oil.
  7. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until golden brown and just cooked through. Remove toothpicks, serve.
